# 📜 Alcheme: Product Requirement Document (PRD)

---

**Project Name:** Alcheme

**Vision:** *Refine fragments, awaken true self.* 

**Core Philosophy:** *What you do and how you think build who you are. Define yourself, beyond the rules.* (We don't set the criteria; you are the alchemist of your own life.)

---

## 1. Background & Pain Points (The "Why")

### 1.1 "Data Exile" in the Web2 Era

- **Data Silos & Asset Loss:** Our growth data (reading habits, fitness, coding commits) is trapped within giant centralized applications. Users do not truly own the "sovereignty" of these personal growth assets.
- **Fragmented Self-Perception:** It is difficult for individuals to see a multi-dimensional, authentic, and evolving version of themselves. Tiny milestones (e.g., studying Solidity for 3 days) vanish instantly without positive feedback, leading to a sense of nihilism and burnout.

### 1.2 The "Vertical Oppression" of Social Labels

- **Hyper-Labeling:** Traditional resumes and social media force us to be "vertical" and "labeled" (e.g., Manager, Software Engineer).
- **Erasure of Multi-dimensional Beings:** Society demands "professionalism," but humans are complex. Slash-youth and cross-disciplinary enthusiasts need a tool to break free from pre-defined frameworks and customize their identities. **Who you are is defined solely by you.**

### 1.3 Solution: The Alchemy Logic

Utilizing AI to extract "spiritual sparks" from daily fragments and condensing them into immutable **SBT (Soulbound Token)** badges via blockchain technology. This is not a resume; it is a "Digital Mirror" reflecting your evolving, authentic self.

---

## 2. Core Product Logic (The Alchemy Pipeline)

We have constructed an evolutionary chain: **"Ore → Card → Badge,"** emphasizing **"Personal Sovereignty"** and **"Visualized Growth."**

- **Collecting (Ore):** Record any small task, thought, or micro-progress.
    - *Sovereignty:* No matter how small the event, if you recognize its value, it is a shining "Ore."
- **Refining (Card):** When several Ores accumulate into a milestone (or even one high-impact Ore), they can be smelted into a **"Milestone Card."**
    - *Visual Incentive:* AI generates exquisite artistic imagery based on the content.
- **Awakening (Badge):** Multiple Cards are synthesized into an **"Identity Badge"** (e.g., "Web3 Explorer").
    - *On-chain Proof:* Badges are minted as SBTs, encapsulating all underlying Ore and Card metadata.
- **Evolution (Continuous Growth):** Identity is not static. You can inject new Cards into existing Badges, allowing your on-chain identity to evolve alongside you.

## 3. Functional Modules (The 4-Page Demo)

### Page 1: Collect - The Inspiration Mine

- **Interaction A: Manual Mining.** Users input voice or text; AI real-time summarizes and extracts core insights to generate **Ores**.
- **Interaction B: External Letters.** Simulates data ingestion from external apps (Mock). Click the "Letter" to collect external achievements as Ores, breaking data silos.

### Page 2: Refine - The Alchemist’s Cauldron

- **Visuals:** A central burning cauldron with a "Crystal Cabinet" on the side storing all collected Ores.
- **Action:** Select Ores from the cabinet and click "Refine."
- **Output:** AI generates summary text and a stunning **Card Illustration**. Users can fine-tune the content, maintaining total creative control.

### Page 3: Awaken - The Mystic Portal

- **Visuals:** A mysterious door leading to the inner self, displaying all earned Cards and Badges.
- **Action:** Select multiple Cards to "Awaken" a new Badge, or select an existing Badge and add new Cards to **Evolve** it.
- **Core Logic:** Badges are the proof of identity and the distillation of your growth trajectory.

### Page 4: Profile - The Badge Wall & Hexagonal Mirror

- **Badge Wall:** The core external-facing profile. Clicking any Badge reveals the historical Ores and Cards behind it, creating a transparent, immutable **Proof of Growth**.
- **6-Dimensional Radar Chart:**
    - *Logic:* Users define their own dimensions (e.g., Wisdom, Grit, Perception). AI scores each Ore based on content.
    - *Philosophy:* Rejecting one-dimensional "rat races," encouraging users to become self-defined "Hexagonal Warriors."

## 4. Technical Specifications (Demo Version)

- **Frontend Paradigm:** **AI-Driven Component Synthesis.** We have moved beyond traditional "pixel-level manual coding," utilizing a **Figma + Gemini 3** hybrid workflow. UI logic is driven directly via natural language instructions.
- **Core Architecture:** **React / Next.js** with **Tailwind CSS** for styling.
- **UI Aesthetic:** **"Light Alchemist Witch"** style. A palette of Purple, Lavender, White, and Gold—fresh, mysterious, and supplemented by cute, toon-style alchemical elements.
- **AI Engine (Dual-Model Strategy):**
    - **LLM:** OpenAI GPT-4o (Semantic extraction and scoring).
    - **Image Gen:** gpt-image-1 / DALL-E (Artistic card generation).
- **Blockchain:** **ERC-721** (SBT Mode). Ores and Cards are stored off-chain/locally, while final Badge metadata is bundled and minted on-chain.

## 5. The Alcheme Difference

1. **Unstructured Freedom:** No levels, no templates. Your growth is defined by your own timing and quantity.
2. **Anti-Comparison:** The Radar Chart and Badge Wall are internal feedback loops for personal evolution, emphasizing the richness of the self rather than social hierarchy.
3. **Traceable Truth:** Utilizing Web3 to ensure every "Glory" (Badge) is backed by "Grit" (Ore).

## 💡 Demo Stage Considerations

- **Anti-Abuse Logic:** While we encourage freedom, future versions will implement **"Community Consensus"** or **"AI Consistency Checks"** to prevent spamming.
- **SBT Latency:** To counter on-chain indexing delays, the Demo uses **"Instant Visual Feedback"** (immediate UI glow/growth effects) to ensure a smooth user experience.
- **Data Ingestion:** Mock data is used to demonstrate the potential of "Universal Data Integration," proving our commitment to breaking Web2 silos.
